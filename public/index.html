TYPEWRITING TAMIL

COA

The Computer on Office Automation (COA) examination is conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education (DOTE) in Tamil Nadu. To qualify, candidates must have completed a typewriting course, either at the junior or senior level.

Course Details

TALLY

The Tally examination is essential for those looking to establish a career in accounting and finance. Our institute offers comprehensive training to prepare candidates for Tally certification, enhancing their employability in various sectors
