Fee Structure

To enroll in the course, a total fee of ₹600 is required, which includes a ₹500 course fee and a ₹100 admission fee. Ensure the fee is paid on the day of enrollment.

Fee is same for both the junior and senior grade.

Exam Requirements for Tamil Typing Course Junior

Paper I

To successfully complete the typing exam, students must meet the following criteria:
Typing Speed: 30 words per minute (WPM).
Total Strokes: A total of 1,500 strokes must be completed.
Time Limit: The exam must be completed within 10 minutes.


Paper II

Content: The exam will consist of letter and statement.
Total Marks: 100 marks
Duration: 45 minutes

Exam Requirements for Tamil Typing Course Senior

Paper I

To successfully complete the typing exam, students must meet the following criteria:
Typing Speed: 45 words per minute (WPM).
Total Strokes: A total of 2,250 strokes must be completed.
Time Limit: The exam must be completed within 10 minutes.


Paper II

Content: The exam will consist of letter and statement.
Total Marks: 100 marks
Duration: 60 minutes
